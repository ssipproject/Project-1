In 2020, we encountered an inspiring group of artisans and an idea was born: a way to combine our passion for fashion with a desire to help those in need. Today, madewithLuv continues to play a major role in the lives of indigenous artists, who are finding economic independence through the increasing popularity of their beautiful style. Also we are collaboration with UNICEF to Open Donation for COVID-19, as we can see all the people around the world are attacked by corona virus. The potential for superspreader events underscores the importance of physical distancing, including avoiding gathering in large groups, to control spread of COVID-19. Enhancing community awareness can encourage symptomatic persons and contacts of ill persons to isolate or self-quarantine to prevent ongoing transmission.
